Recipe: Classic Pancakes
Category: breakfast
Cuisine: american
Tags: quick, vegetarian, comfort-food, pancake

Ingredients:
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 egg
- 3/4 cup milk
- 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions:
1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
2. In another bowl, whisk egg, milk, and melted butter.
3. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
4. Heat a lightly greased pan over medium heat.
5. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake.
6. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden brown.

Cooking Time: 15 minutes

Recipe: Spaghetti Aglio e Olio
Category: dinner
Cuisine: italian
Tags: quick, vegetarian, pasta, garlic

Ingredients:
- 200g spaghetti
- 4 garlic cloves, sliced
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
- Fresh parsley, chopped
- Salt to taste

Instructions:
1. Cook spaghetti according to package directions.
2. Heat olive oil in a pan.
3. Add garlic and cook until golden.
4. Stir in red pepper flakes.
5. Toss cooked spaghetti with the garlic oil.
6. Garnish with parsley and serve.

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Recipe: Vegetable Fried Rice
Category: dinner
Cuisine: asian
Tags: quick, vegetarian, stir-fry, rice

Ingredients:
- 2 cups cooked rice
- 1 cup mixed vegetables
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon sesame oil
- 2 green onions, chopped

Instructions:
1. Scramble eggs in a wok and set aside.
2. Stir-fry vegetables for 3–4 minutes.
3. Add rice and mix well.
4. Stir in soy sauce and sesame oil.
5. Add eggs back to the wok.
6. Garnish with green onions.

Cooking Time: 15 minutes

Recipe: Tomato Basil Soup
Category: lunch
Cuisine: italian
Tags: vegetarian, soup, comfort-food

Ingredients:
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
- 2 cups vegetable broth
- 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Heat olive oil in a pot over medium heat.
2. Sauté onion and garlic until softened, about 5 minutes.
3. Add crushed tomatoes and vegetable broth.
4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes.
5. Stir in basil and season with salt and pepper.
6. Serve hot, optionally with crusty bread.

Cooking Time: 25 minutes

Recipe: Grilled Cheese Sandwich
Category: lunch
Cuisine: american
Tags: quick, vegetarian, comfort-food, sandwich

Ingredients:
- 2 slices bread
- 2 slices cheddar cheese
- 1 tablespoon butter

Instructions:
1. Butter one side of each bread slice.
2. Place cheese between the unbuttered sides of the bread.
3. Heat a pan over medium-low heat.
4. Cook sandwich until golden brown on one side, about 3 minutes.
5. Flip and cook until the other side is golden and cheese is melted.
6. Cut in half and serve immediately.

Cooking Time: 10 minutes

Recipe: Chicken Stir Fry
Category: dinner
Cuisine: asian
Tags: high-protein, stir-fry, quick

Ingredients:
- 1 lb chicken breast, sliced into strips
- 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell pepper, broccoli, carrots)
- 3 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 1 teaspoon cornstarch

Instructions:
1. Mix soy sauce, honey, garlic, and cornstarch in a small bowl.
2. Heat oil in a wok or large pan over high heat.
3. Stir-fry chicken until cooked through, about 5–6 minutes. Set aside.
4. Stir-fry vegetables for 3–4 minutes until crisp-tender.
5. Return chicken to the pan and pour in the sauce.
6. Toss everything together and cook for 1–2 minutes until sauce thickens.

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Recipe: Banana Smoothie
Category: breakfast
Cuisine: american
Tags: quick, drink, vegetarian, no-cook, sweet

Ingredients:
- 2 ripe bananas
- 1 cup milk
- 1/2 cup yogurt
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 4 ice cubes

Instructions:
1. Peel bananas and break into chunks.
2. Add bananas, milk, yogurt, honey, and vanilla to a blender.
3. Add ice cubes.
4. Blend on high until smooth and creamy.
5. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.

Cooking Time: 5 minutes

Recipe: Margherita Pizza
Category: dinner
Cuisine: italian
Tags: vegetarian, baked, comfort-food

Ingredients:
- 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
- 1/2 cup tomato sauce
- 8 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced
- Fresh basil leaves
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- Salt to taste

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface.
3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
4. Arrange mozzarella slices on top.
5. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt.
6. Bake for 12–15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
7. Top with fresh basil before serving.

Cooking Time: 25 minutes

Recipe: Beef Tacos
Category: dinner
Cuisine: mexican
Tags: high-protein, quick, spicy

Ingredients:
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 packet taco seasoning
- 8 taco shells
- 1 cup shredded lettuce
- 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 1 tomato, diced
- Sour cream and salsa for serving

Instructions:
1. Brown ground beef in a skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Drain excess fat and stir in taco seasoning with 1/4 cup water.
3. Simmer for 5 minutes until thickened.
4. Warm taco shells according to package directions.
5. Fill shells with beef, lettuce, cheese, and tomato.
6. Serve with sour cream and salsa.

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Recipe: Greek Salad
Category: salad
Cuisine: greek
Tags: quick, salad, vegetarian, no-cook, mediterranean

Ingredients:
- 2 cups chopped romaine lettuce
- 1 cucumber, diced
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
- 1/2 cup Kalamata olives
- 4 oz feta cheese, crumbled
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano

Instructions:
1. Combine lettuce, cucumber, tomatoes, onion, and olives in a large bowl.
2. Whisk olive oil, vinegar, and oregano in a small bowl.
3. Pour dressing over the salad and toss gently.
4. Top with crumbled feta cheese.
5. Serve immediately.

Cooking Time: 15 minutes

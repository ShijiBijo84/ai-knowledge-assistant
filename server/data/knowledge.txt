Recipe: Classic Pancakes

Ingredients:
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 egg
- 3/4 cup milk
- 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions:
1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
2. In another bowl, whisk egg, milk, and melted butter.
3. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
4. Heat a lightly greased pan over medium heat.
5. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake.
6. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden brown.

Cooking Time: 15 minutes

Recipe: Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Ingredients:
- 200g spaghetti
- 4 garlic cloves, sliced
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
- Fresh parsley, chopped
- Salt to taste

Instructions:
1. Cook spaghetti according to package directions.
2. Heat olive oil in a pan.
3. Add garlic and cook until golden.
4. Stir in red pepper flakes.
5. Toss cooked spaghetti with the garlic oil.
6. Garnish with parsley and serve.

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Recipe: Vegetable Fried Rice

Ingredients:
- 2 cups cooked rice
- 1 cup mixed vegetables
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon sesame oil
- 2 green onions, chopped

Instructions:
1. Scramble eggs in a wok and set aside.
2. Stir-fry vegetables for 3–4 minutes.
3. Add rice and mix well.
4. Stir in soy sauce and sesame oil.
5. Add eggs back to the wok.
6. Garnish with green onions.

Cooking Time: 15 minutes

Recipe: Chocolate Mug Cake

Ingredients:
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 3 tablespoons milk
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions:
1. Mix all ingredients in a microwave-safe mug.
2. Stir until smooth.
3. Microwave for 60–90 seconds.
4. Let cool slightly before serving.

Cooking Time: 5 minutes

Recipe: Caesar Salad

Ingredients:
- 1 head romaine lettuce
- 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- Croutons
- 3 tablespoons Caesar dressing

Instructions:
1. Wash and chop lettuce.
2. Toss with dressing.
3. Add Parmesan cheese and croutons.
4. Serve immediately.

Cooking Time: 10 minutes
